Thrift

    Duo together with: Lukas Arnold

    • • Personal Project 1 at Playgroundsquad.
    • • Duration: 4 Weeks.
    • • Contribution: Designed Gameplay & Level Design systems, UI & UX.
    • • Platform: Developed for PC using Unreal 5.
    • • Features: Stealth mechancis such as noise, body detection & alertness. Free climbing onto edges. A multi-purpose gun which can pacify guards, create platforms and press buttons from long distance. Stylized graphics via Post-Processing Shaders and more!
    • • Challenges: Plannning & Structuring a short project. Deeper learning into the Unreal architecture and built-in systems.
    • Game Page: TBA

    • Thrift is a love letter to classic stealth games such as Dishonored & Thief with our own personal twist on it. With noir-style graphics, movement systems in the form of ledge vaulting, platform-making and tight vents to climb through & sophisticated (albeit slightly blind) guards Thrift is a passion project which we hope to expand upon post our designated time from the school.
