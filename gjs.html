    • Together with one of my peers I hosted & participated in a 48h Gamejam @ Playgroundsquad. The game I contributed with was called Lost Memories and was made in collaboration with Lukas Arnold
      • .
      In Lost Memories the player finds a phone on the sidewalk. Curious as to what is may contain they unlock it and snoop through the life of an indivudal whose life runs parallel with their own.

Contribution & Reflection

    Lost memories was made in under 48h using almost entierly Blueprint scripting within Unreal Engine's UI framework UMG. The functionality of the game is there with code-locked apps, messages and pictures. Due to time restraints there are some huge bugs which make the game difficult to complete as well as a huge narrative portion of the game was not able to be implemented in time.

    I learnt a lot about how to structure UI elements in Unreal with this project and I would say that it has carried over nicely into projects afterwards.
