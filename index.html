About me

Tja! I am, as my beautiful title suggests; Calle Wahlstedt Nilsson! I am a 21 year old Game Designer from Sweden with the sole goal of creating games that act as memorable experiences. I am currently looking for a Game Design LIA-Internship position for Autumn 2023 - Summer 2024.
