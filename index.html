About me

My name is Calle, and I am an aspiring Gameplay and UI designer currently interning at Aurora Arts Studio in Skövde. I am currently scouting for jobs related to video game design around in Sweden!


I am a passionate Game Designer with many years of game analysis behind and one year of development experience in Game Design behind me. Scripting as well as Project & Team Management are some of my favourite tasks within my field. Whenever I design games I try to mix writing and gameplay together to form a coherent ludonarrative where immersion and player agency comes first.


If there are any questions about me or what I do; please keep looking around this page, or send me a message!
