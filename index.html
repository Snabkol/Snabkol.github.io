About me

My name is Calle, and I am an aspiring Gameplay and UI designer currently enrolled at Playgroundsquad in Sweden. I am currently looking for an internship or Junior position from November 2023.

I am a passionate Game Designer with many years of game analysis behind and one year of development experience in Game Design behind me. Scripting as well as Project & Team Management are some of my favourite tasks within my field. Whenever I design games I try to mix writing and gameplay together to form a coherent ludonarrative where immersion and player agency comes first.

If there are any questions about me or what I do; please keep looking around this page, or send me a message!

Projects

  • Thrift is a stealth platformer akin to Dishonored or the Thief games. With a free vaulting system, a unique blend of platforming and stealth mechanics make the game feel intuitive while the stylized graphics hook the player in!

  • Alien Intern is a Comedic Module Puzzle game developed for the PSVR2 in which your job is to complete a plethora of meanial, stressful tasks to be able to buy out your roommate so that you may live in peace

  • Trouble in Loomie Land is a 3D Platformer where you roam Loomie Land to find all the bottled love. Can you find all the bottles and save the Loomies from a gloomy fate?!

  • Call of Dinner is a First-person shooter mixed with a cooking simulator where you rack up points by killing vegetables, scooping up their corpses and use them to cook succulent meals.
