Project Overview

Summary

Thrift started of as a personal project during mine and my friend Lukas Arnold's time at Playgroundsquad. Both of us knew that we wanted to make something bigger than we could on our own; so we decided to collaborate.

The goal was to create a small vertical gameplay slice taking inspiration from some of our favourite games: Dishonored & Thief.

We ended up with a game where the player has a lot of freedom in their movement. They can vault over any ledge and are equipped with a gun capable of placing platform on any surface.

For the project I was doing most of the programming in Blueprints and focused my design on Gameplay & UI. Below you will find a handful of the systems I made for the game.

  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Team Size: 2
